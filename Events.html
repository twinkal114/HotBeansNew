Web Development Workshop

Hot Beans Web regularly runs web development workshops for beginners who want to learn the fundamentals of website creation. These workshops introduce technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and allow participants to practice building simple web pages.

Internship Opportunities

Hot Beans Web offers internship placements for individuals who want to gain real industry experience working in a professional web development company.

Interns work alongside our development team and assist with real projects including website design, front-end development and testing.

This opportunities is ideal for students, graduates and self-taught developers who want to build their portfolio and improve their technical skills.
